About

Metallica is a heavy metal band. Reigning from Los Angeles in California,
the 4 man band holds 9 grammy's from it's 23 nominations. Asides from
founding the 'big four' bands of thrash metal, which includes
Slayer, Megadeth and Anthrax, Metallica was inducted into the
Rock and Roll Hall of Fame back in 2009.

The website features a playlist section, in which you can create a custom
playlist to play & listen too in your browser, or simply fully preview each song.
If you wished to purchase the music there is a link to Spotify in Social where they
are fully available.

Furthermore is the Demo section, in this there is a video displaying the features
and functionality of this website.
